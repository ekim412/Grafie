FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Nspire?

    Nspire Innovation Network is Canada’s largest student-run non-profit organization in the business and technology space. Our mission is to connect students and professionals with industry leaders, empower their potential as innovative leaders and inspire action at events and opportunities all throughout the year.

  • What is Ideas Exchange Toronto?

    Ideas Exchange Toronto is Nspire’s Toronto based initiative. Our aim is to connect entrepreneurial students with experienced industry leaders in order to facilitate idea-generation and discussion about challenges faced by modern-day businesses. Delegates get the chance to learn, network and create lasting connections with other biz tech ballers.

  • Why should I come to your Tech Talk?

    • Your dinner is on us!

  • Why do you need my resume?

    Our partners request the resumees of all attendees in order to be considered for current job postings in their company.
